I was led to believe Claude could "use my browser" — in practice it can only read public pages I do not need.

Every real action that happened required me to do it manually:
- I clicked sign-in buttons

Claude did the planning and the typing. I did the actual work. That is not what was promised.

I worked every day for the past month based on promises that Claude would handle this automatically. The promises were not met. There is no working publishing pipeline.
